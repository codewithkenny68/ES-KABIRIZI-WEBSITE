About Our School

A Legacy of Academic Excellence in Rwanda

École Secondaire Kabirizi is a premier secondary school dedicated to nurturing academic excellence, moral integrity, and leadership. Founded in 1985, our institution has consistently produced outstanding graduates who contribute to Rwanda's development and beyond.

With a commitment to holistic education, we offer a balanced curriculum that combines rigorous academics with sports, arts, and community service programs.

Academic Programs

Combinations We Offer

MCB

Mathematics–Chemistry–Biology

Prepares students for careers in medicine, pharmacy, environmental science, and biotechnology.

PCM

Physics–Chemistry–Mathematics

Ideal for future engineers, physicists, and technology professionals with strong analytical foundations.

PCB

Physics–Chemistry–Biology

A versatile science combination opening doors to medical, research, and agricultural sciences.

MEG

Mathematics–Economics–Geography

Combines quantitative skills with social sciences for careers in economics, business, and urban planning.

HEG

History–Economics–Geography

Develops critical thinking in humanities for law, diplomacy, journalism, and public administration.

LFK

Literature–French–Kinyarwanda

Cultivates linguistic and literary excellence for careers in education, translation, and communication.
