Our History

A journey of growth and excellence.

ES Kabirizi was established with a vision to provide quality education rooted in discipline, science, and moral values. Over the years, the school has grown into a strong academic institution serving students from different backgrounds.

From its early beginnings, the school has continuously improved its teaching methods, infrastructure, and student support systems to ensure every learner reaches their full potential.

What sets us apart

A complete school community built for success.

Strong academics

At ES Kabirizi, students learn through a thoughtful curriculum that emphasizes science, mathematics, languages, and civic education. Our teachers support each learner with clear explanations, practical examples, and regular study support.

Care and guidance

We create a caring school environment where discipline is balanced with encouragement. Students receive mentorship, character development, and guidance to help them grow into confident young people.

Facilities and activities

The campus includes classrooms, outdoor spaces, and room for extracurricular activities. Clubs, sports, and community projects help learners build leadership, teamwork, and practical life skills.

Community focus

ES Kabirizi is rooted in the local community, and we encourage students to show respect, responsibility, and service. Parents and neighbors are invited to partner with the school so every child can succeed.
